Who We Are

What started as a collaboration between two friends, Nitesh V. Ranglani & Parzaan Dastur, took the shape of a dependable team that grows stronger with each project

Their maiden project, Pocket Mummy (Short Film) garnered official recognition at the 8th Dada Saheb Phalke Film Festival, 2018.

The journey spans from digital promos to emotional music videos across industries like finance, fashion, hospitality, and more.

The tale continues as we explore new genres. The future is exciting and filled with movie magic!
